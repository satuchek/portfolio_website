Pass On, Ghostie

Pass On, Ghostie was a game that I created with a team during the 2018 Global Game Jam. We decided to create a game about a ghost that must possess others' bodies, and die in them, in order to interact with the physical world. I was responsible for implementing the systems that allow the ghost to possess bodies as well as the way the gravestones interact with the world. You can view the trailer on Youtube or see our page on the Global Game Jam site by clicking the icons to the right!

Girs Who Code: Cobb County

In the fall of 2018, I became the lead facilitator for Cobb County Library System's Girls Who Code club. We are now in our second semester and I have worked with my co-faciliators to create a curriculum for teaching middle and high-school girls a variety of computer science topics. We introduce them to robotics, web development, as well as programming in Python. At the end of each semester, the girls build a project to help the community. You can see news about us by clicking on the icon to the right!

Liquid Courage

Liquid courage is a game I created with a team in Fall 2017 for the KSU Fall Game Jam. The object of the game is to lead low-health villagers through a dangerous dungeon by placing barriers, bridges, and crates. I was responsible for programming the objects to be draggable onto the play area from the UI. I was also responsible for making the background parallax. You can check out our trailer by clicking the Youtube link to the right or download a demo by following the Github link!
